Our Mission

Ocutherapy offers virtual reality guided rehabilitation - made easy.
Being user centered, accessible, and targeted, Ocutherapy focuses on brain injury effects
by offering interactive therapeutic tasks and being available to those who need it most.

Contact US



Ocutherapy is complementary therapy

Ease of Access


Ocutherapy is simple to use; offering clearly illustrated and guided experiences making rehabilitation always within arms-reach.

Immersive


Using dynamic gameplay, Ocutherapy adapts to the evolving needs of the user in real-time, creating a truly connected therapeutic experience.

Targeted


With each use, Ocutherapy learns to identify and better assist users by simply interacting with their environment, bridging the gap between game and therapy.

Our Team

Alex Theodorou
Founder

Clinical researcher, now entrepreneur, and life- long learner. Drawing from my background in Cognitive science, Neuroscience and Linguistics, my passion is to combine these experiences in new and exciting ways developing approaches so we can better improve the lives of those suffering from cognitive impairments. With my father one of those living with the life long effects of a brain injury, my goal is to strengthen, inspire and motivate those throughout this therapeutic journey. I embed these principles into all aspects of Ocutherapy as well as in my personal pursuit within my research goals.
