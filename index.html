Our Mission Statement

Ocutherapy offers virtual reality guided rehabilitation - made easy.
Being user centered, accessible, and targeted, Ocutherapy focuses on brain injury effects
by offering interactive theraputic tasks and being available to those who need it most.

Ease of Access

Ocutherapy is simple to use; offering clearly illustrated and guided experiences, users can start, stop, or continue their therapeutic experience as they please making rehabilitation always within arms-reach.

Immersive

Using dynamic gameplay, Ocutherapy provides real-time, user-generated changes in tasks, resulting in novel experiences for each exercise which collects concise and predictive performance measurements..

Targeted

Using the leading source for visual rendering, Ocutherapy creates a virtual environment not seen in any other virtual experiences in therapy. This offers a means for users to fully connect and interact in the virtual tasks, bridging the gap between game and therapy.
